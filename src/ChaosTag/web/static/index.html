Instructions:

How to play:

  • Use arrow keys or "WASD" to move your player around the map and get bigger.
  • You, the blue circle, can get bigger by eating food and eliminating players.
  • Eliminate players of a smaller size by chasing them and consuming them.
  • Once one player reaches critical mass, that player wins, and a new game begins.
  • Note: Food is worth .1 "mass", and players are worth 25% of eliminated players "mass"
